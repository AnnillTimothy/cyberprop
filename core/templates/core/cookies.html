Cookies Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

CyberProp uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They include:

  • Session cookies: Maintain your login state and session information.
  • CSRF tokens: Protect against cross-site request forgery attacks.
  • Cart cookies: Remember items in your shopping cart.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation:

  • Preferences: Remember your language, theme, and display preferences.
  • Form data: Pre-fill form fields for convenience.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve the user experience. These cookies collect anonymous information.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use:

  • PayFast: Payment processing cookies during checkout.
  • CDN providers: Content delivery network cookies for serving static resources.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set preferences for certain types of cookies

Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of CyberProp, including the ability to log in and use the shopping cart.

5. Cookie Duration

Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us.
